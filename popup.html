Getting Started

To get started with the Accessibility Tool, simply navigate to the Home tab and click the "Generate Report" button. This will scan the current page for accessibility issues and generate a report for you to review.

Configuring Your Report

If you want to customize your report, click on the Report Configuration tab. Here, you can select which accessibility checks you want to perform and adjust other settings to meet your needs.

Interpreting Your Results

Once you've generated your report, take the time to review each issue and its severity rating. Some issues may be easy to fix, while others may require more time and effort. Consider consulting with an accessibility expert if you're unsure how to fix a particular issue.
